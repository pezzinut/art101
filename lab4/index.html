Task 1 : pseudocode an everyday task

How to make iced coffee

  1. Get the coffee grounds
  2. Put a filter in the coffee pot
  3. Pour 3 tbsp of coffee into the filter
  4. Pour 3 cups of water into the water compartment
  5. Turn on the coffee pot
  6. Wait for the coffee to brew
  7. Once coffee is brewed, pour until the cup is 3/4 full
  8. Let coffee cool for 10 minutes
  9. Add extras to your coffee , like sugar or cream!
  10. add ice
  11. enjoy!
